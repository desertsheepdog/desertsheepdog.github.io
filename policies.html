Age Requirements

The spa is an adult- oriented atmosphere. Clients must be 18 years old to receive body services. I will provide body services to teens at least 16 years old with parental permission.

Insurance Billing

Payment is expected at time of service. However, I can provide you with a billing statement that you can submit to your insurance company or health savings plan for reimbursement. I accept cash, check and credit cards.

Disclaimer

I do not diagnose illness, disease or any other physical or mental disorder. Massage therapy is given for the purpose of relaxation, stress reduction, relief from muscular tension, spasm and stiff joints, and increased blood flow and circulation. It is the client's responsibility to inform me of all medical issues past and present including skin conditions, sensitivities, disorders, and allergies especially to fragrances, creams and oils.
