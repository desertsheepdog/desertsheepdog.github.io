Massage Therapy

Massage therapy is becoming mainstream in the area of stress reduction, the easing of aches and pains and the improvement of many health issues. The National Certification Board for Therapeutic Massage and Bodywork finds many health benefits from massage including:

  • Reduced blood pressure
  • A stronger immune system
  • Increased circulation
  • Pain, stiffness and chronic back pain relief
  • Increased mobility and speed of the healing process
  • Easier time through pregnancy

With high pressure jobs, over-committed obligations and trying to pack three days of priorities into one itâ€™s no wonder we are overstressed. Massage therapy provides a peaceful and tranquil environment where gentle, medium and firm flowing massage strokes are applied to the skin and muscle tissue. These massage strokes stimulate many of our body systems such as the lymphatic, circulatory and cardiovascular system. As the circulatory system is being stimulated through massage the cardiovascular system is sending highly oxygenated blood throughout the body and skin. Toxins and other waste products being held in the body and muscle tissue are being released and carried through our lymphatic system for elimination.

There are many types of massage that can benefit the body and can help with a myriad of chronic conditions and diseases. Why not give it a try? It may be the most wonderful experiences of your life!
